# Fed Coscolluela Portfolio

A personal website where I share my work, experience, and academic highlights.

## About the Site

This portfolio is designed as a simple and polished way to share my background, research, and accomplishments.

It features:

- A welcoming introduction with my name, role, and interests
- A brief personal summary with education and research highlights
- Featured projects with more details available on click
- A timeline of my professional and volunteer experience
- A mobile-friendly navigation layout for easy viewing

## What You Will Find

### Welcome

A clean landing area that introduces me and the types of digital solutions I focus on.

### About Me

A short summary of my background, including my Computer Science education, healthcare informatics research, and academic awards.

### Projects

Selected work includes:

- A patient monitor transcription project from my thesis work
- A blockchain-based procurement solution honored with a hackathon award
- A heart disease analytics dashboard
- A manufacturing cost automation project recognized with a Best Paper award

Each project card can be clicked to reveal more information and a link to the source or project details.

### Experience

A timeline of my roles and achievements, including positions at Texas Instruments and volunteer leadership with Google Developer Groups.
